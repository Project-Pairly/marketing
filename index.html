Crowdsourced carbon offsets: corporations pay, consumers earn

Problem

By 2030, climate change will have disrupted the food, transportation, and energy sectors. Our everyday lives will be impacted in unpredictable ways.

The Need for Carbon Offsets

Carbon neutrality is an increasing priority for corporations in all sectors, and simply reducing emissions is not enough, especially in industries difficult to decarbonize. Corporations must pay for carbon offsets through third party offset projects.

Drawbacks of Current Solutions

  • Costly – requires time & money for research & projects valuation
  • Lack of transparency – end result is not shown to customers
  • Missing variable – consumers are left out of the equation

Solution

We build a marketplace where corporations’ demand for carbon offsets is met by everyday consumers.

Our Vision

We're building a B2B2C marketplace where corporations’ demand for carbon offsets is met by everyday consumers like their own customers. Individuals earn money by taking carbon negative actions and generating carbon offsets. Corporations buy carbon offsets from our marketplace.

Where We Win

  • Convenient & cost-effective – cheaper and easier than alternatives
  • Increased transparency – discloses corporations’ contributions
  • Social pressure – incentivize carbon friendly decisions from consumers and corporations
