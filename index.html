Consumer carbon offsets – verified and simplified

Problem

By 2030, climate change will have disrupted the food, transportation, and energy sectors. Our everyday lives will be impacted in unpredictable ways.

Consumers are increasingly climate conscious...

Today's consumers, especially Gen Z, care about sustainability and their carbon footprint. Carbon offsets have emerged as an attractive option for consumers to decrease their own emissions.

...But current carbon offset options are lacking

  • Time consuming – requires consumers to track their own footprint
  • Non-transparent – no insight given into the process of offsetting
  • High in activation energy – no motivation or satisfaction in offsetting

Solution

A new trusted B2B2C sustainability platform.

One-click offset button

  • Easy – offset your transaction directly through checkout
  • Fast – tracks your footprint for you
  • Effective – finds a verified offset project to support with your contribution

Transactions web platform

  • Transparent – track and verify all your contributions to date
  • Satisfying – earn rewards and share with your friends
  • Accountable – view corporations’ contribution history
